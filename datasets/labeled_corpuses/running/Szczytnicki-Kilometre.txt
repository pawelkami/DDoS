The running route in Szczytniki Park (park Szczytnicki) measures 1 km. The route is metalled along its entire length (beyond a 20 m section). The 'Szczytnicki kilometre' can be used for recreational running and advanced training. The route passes the wooden church of St John of Nepomuk. The church dates from the turn of the 17th century.
