The running route between Frog Bridge (Żabia Kładka) and Opatowice Weir (Jaz Opatowicki) measures around 9.5 km. Route surface is varied: from the zoo to Opatowice Weir it is metalled path, from the Opatowice side mainly asphalt. The route is not marked. A panorama of the Oder (Odra) can be admired from Frog Bridge, and the picturesque meanders of the Oława river contemplated at the Opatowice end.
